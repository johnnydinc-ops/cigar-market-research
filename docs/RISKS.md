# Risks and Objections

**In brief:** Top five: (1) weak or unvalidated pain for “combined” flow, (2) retailer and data dependence, (3) affiliate economics, (4) trust if perceived as biased, (5) incumbent response. Mitigations: validate first; diversify data sources and retailers; rank by price/fit and disclose; differentiate on trust and wedge.

---

## Demand and Customer

| Risk | Description | Mitigation |
|------|-------------|------------|
| **Weak customer pain** | “Similar + best price” is nice-to-have, not must-have; users won’t switch from current habits. | Validate with interviews and concierge test: would you use this weekly/monthly? |
| **Heavy brand loyalty** | Most buyers only buy one brand or one retailer; discovery and comparison don’t matter. | Segment focus: target enthusiasts and price-sensitive; measure share of market that explores. |
| **One-off use only** | Users come once per purchase, never return; no retention, only SEO-driven volume. | Accept as possible model; optimize for conversion and SEO; test retention in MVP. |
| **Cigar buyers don’t want discovery from a third party** | They trust retailer or forum only. | Position as utility (price) first; add recommendation as enhancement; measure trust in tests. |

---

## Competitive and Market

| Risk | Description | Mitigation |
|------|-------------|------------|
| **Incumbents do both** | CigarFinder (or someone) adds strong “similar” and owns combined space. | Move fast on wedge; differentiate on trust and UX; consider niche (e.g., premium only). |
| **Users prefer separate tools** | Cigar Sense for rec + CigarFinder for price is “good enough.” | Validate “one place” in interviews; measure satisfaction with combined flow in test. |
| **Category is too niche** | Not enough volume for venture-scale; only affiliate/content scale. | Size the opportunity; be willing to position as niche or lifestyle business, not venture. |

---

## Supply and Data

| Risk | Description | Mitigation |
|------|-------------|------------|
| **Retailer dependence** | We depend on retailer affiliate programs and product/price access; they can change terms or block. | Diversify retailers; prefer official feeds; don’t rely on scraping. |
| **Data quality** | Wrong or stale prices; bad “similar” logic; user loses trust. | Clear “price may vary”; link through; human review for rec logic; start with small catalog. |
| **Scraping fragility** | If we ever rely on scraping, blocks or ToS changes break us. | **Do not rely on scraping.** Use affiliate, feeds, manual curation, partnerships. |
| **Can’t get catalog/attributes** | No sustainable way to build and maintain cigar taxonomy. | Start manual; explore licensing (e.g., Cigar Sense–style); use public content with attribution. |

---

## Monetization and Economics

| Risk | Description | Mitigation |
|------|-------------|------------|
| **Affiliate economics don’t work** | Conversion or commission too low; we can’t cover acquisition and ops. | Model with conservative conversion and blended commission; validate with MVP traffic. |
| **Retailers restrict or ban** | Some retailers don’t want to be in comparison or limit affiliate by state. | Comply with state rules; diversify; don’t depend on one retailer. |
| **Trust vs. revenue** | Maximizing short-term revenue (e.g., pushing high-commission retailers) hurts trust and long-term use. | Policy: rank by price/fit; disclose affiliate; no pay-to-rank in core results. |

---

## Legal and Compliance

| Risk | Description | Mitigation |
|------|-------------|------------|
| **State-by-state tobacco** | Shipping and marketing rules vary; platform could be implicated. | Don’t sell or ship; we refer. Age-gate or disclaimer if advised by counsel; document state restrictions for affiliates. |
| **PACT Act** | Age verification and shipping are retailer responsibilities. | We don’t fulfill; ensure we don’t facilitate underage access; legal review for any age-related UX. |
| **FTC / affiliate disclosure** | Must disclose affiliate relationship. | Clear disclosure on site and near links; follow FTC guidance. |
| **ToS and data use** | Using retailer or third-party data in ways that violate ToS or law. | Only use data from permitted sources (affiliate, license, public with attribution); no scraping against ToS. |

---

## Product and Trust

| Risk | Description | Mitigation |
|------|-------------|------------|
| **Trust and bias** | Users believe we steer to high-commission or sponsored results. | Transparent policy; no pay-to-rank; label sponsored; publish how we rank. |
| **Recommendation quality** | “Similar” is wrong or generic; users ignore or leave. | Start with conservative, attribute-based logic; iterate with feedback; don’t overclaim. |
| **Users just buy from favorite retailer** | Habit and loyalty trump comparison. | Target segments that already compare or explore; don’t assume we change hard-core loyalists. |

---

## Summary: Top Risks

1. **Weak or unvalidated pain** — “Combined rec + deal” may not be important enough. **Validate first.**
2. **Retailer and data dependence** — Affiliate and data access can change. **Diversify; no scraping.**
3. **Affiliate economics** — May not support the business at scale. **Model and measure in MVP.**
4. **Trust** — If we’re seen as biased, we lose users. **Design for transparency from day one.**
5. **Incumbent response** — CigarFinder or others could close the gap. **Differentiate on trust and wedge; move with focus.**
