## Suitability for Price Monitoring and Data

| Approach | Feasibility | Risk / note |
|----------|-------------|-------------|
| **Affiliate feeds / APIs** | Varies by retailer | Preferred if available; check CJ, direct programs for product/price feeds. |
| **Manual curation (MVP)** | High | Legal; sustainable for small catalog; doesn’t scale. |
| **Scraping** | Often against ToS; brittle | **Not recommended.** Can trigger blocks; legal and ethical issues. |
| **Partnership / data deal** | Possible long-term | Approach retailers with clear value prop (traffic, conversion); no assumption of access. |

**Principle:** Prefer lawful, sustainable methods. Where scraping is risky or forbidden, say so and use affiliate data, feeds, or limited manual curation instead.

## Compliance and Ethics

- **PACT Act / shipping:** Age verification and shipping rules are retailer responsibilities when the user checks out on their site. Our platform should not facilitate underage access; consider age-gate or disclaimer on entry if appropriate (legal review recommended).
- **State restrictions:** Some retailers (e.g., CI) limit affiliate by state. Honor those limits.
- **Disclosure:** Affiliate relationship must be disclosed where required (FTC, platform policies).
- **Data use:** Only use data obtained through permitted means (affiliate, partnership, public catalog). Do not scrape in violation of ToS.
